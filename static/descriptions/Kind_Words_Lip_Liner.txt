A super creamy, waterproof lip liner that glides on like a balm to define lips—in the same 10 rich, made-to-match shades as the Kind Words Matte Lipstick.

Callouts:
Free of parabens, formaldehydes, formaldehyde-releasing agents, phthalates, oxybenzone, coal tar, hydroquinone, sulfates SLS & SLES, triclocarban, and triclosan. It is also vegan, cruelty-free, and comes in recyclable packaging.

Ingredients:
Trimethylsiloxysilicate, Isohexadecane, Synthetic Wax, Mica, Isododecane, Ethylhexyl Palmitate, Microcrystalline Wax/Cera Microcristallina/Cire microcristalline, Polybutene, Silica, C13-15 Alkane, VP/Hexadecene Copolymer, Polyglyceryl-2 Triisostearate, Copernicia Cerifera (Carnauba) Wax/Copernicia Cerifera Cera/Cire de carnauba, Paraffin, Ethylene/Propylene Copolymer, Fragrance/Parfum, Caprylyl Glycol, Diisostearyl Malate, Gardenia Florida Fruit Extract, Glyceryl Caprylate, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Nelumbo Nucifera Flower Extract, Nymphaea Odorata Root Extract, Polyglyceryl-2 Diisostearate, Linalool. +/- (May Contain/Peut Contenir) Titanium Dioxide (CI 77891), Blue 1 Lake (CI 42090), Iron Oxides (CI 77491, CI 77492, CI 77499), Red 7 Lake (CI 15850).

Usage:
-Twist up to reveal product.
-Outline lips starting from the corner of the top lip.
-Fill in lips using a corresponding lipstick shade—Rare Beauty by Selena Gomez recommends pairing with the Kind Words Matte Lipstick in a matching shade (sold separately).
-For an amped-up look that lasts even longer, use lip liner to color in your lips before applying lipstick.
